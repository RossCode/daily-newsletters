### [Anthropic Scales Claude Mythos to Critical Infrastructure in 15 Countries](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/02/anthropic-scales-claude-mythos-to-critical-infrastructure-in-15-countries/)

*(1 newsletter)*

Anthropic is expanding Project Glasswing, with Claude Mythos at its center, to roughly 150 organizations across more than 15 countries. The newly expanded list of participating organizations spans power grids, water systems, healthcare networks, communications infrastructure, and hardware supply chains. This marks a significant escalation in Anthropic's push to deploy its most capable model in high-stakes real-world environments.
